About Vayam Global Exim

Vayam Global Exim is being developed as a professional export platform for Indian food and grocery products. The company is based in Bengaluru, a major commercial hub with access to suppliers across Karnataka, South India and wider Indian food supply chains.

The business objective is simple: help global buyers source Indian products with clarity, reliability and consistent communication. In the first phase, the focus is on grocery staples, spices, food ingredients and selected packaged goods.

Mission: To make Indian grocery sourcing easier for international buyers.
Vision: To build a trusted export brand for Indian food products from Bengaluru.
Approach: Source carefully, document clearly, package responsibly and communicate transparently.

Export workflow

This is the proposed working model for handling international buyer enquiries in a structured way.

Enquiry

Buyer shares product list, quantity, destination, packaging and target price expectation.

Sourcing

We check supplier availability, minimum order quantity, product specification and lead time.

Quotation

Pricing, packaging, payment terms and shipment options are discussed transparently.

Documentation

Invoice, packing list, certificates and buyer-specific documentation are coordinated.

Shipment

Goods are packed, dispatched and tracked with regular buyer communication.
